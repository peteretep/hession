Paint and Varnish
Back
Stephen can paint or varnish any class of boat. He can collect your boat and bring it to his dust-free workshop. Depending on the condition of the existing coating, it will either be removed entirely or sanded smooth. Stephen will then begin building up coats of paint.

This process takes time and cannot be rushed. After the appropriate number of coats the coating is left to harden.

For additional grip on slippy decks, Stephen can add a special grip enhancing additive to the final coat.
